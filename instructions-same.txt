When a trial starts, a fixation cross appears.
Stare at the fixation cross.
Then, press the space bar to confirm you're fixating the cross.

A red target will appear on the screen,
You have to make a fast eye movement to it.

Sometimes a second red target will appear,
You have to make a second fast eye movement to it.

Then a fixation cross will reappear. 
A new trial have started.

Be the most precise and fast you can.
